Salt & Pepper Chicken

Description

Chinese style salt & pepper chicken, served with rice and curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken breast
  • 1 chilli
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 10g salt & pepper seasoning

  • 120g rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Curry sauce

Steps

  1. Cut veg & chicken and add to wok. Cook for 10-15 minutes.
  2. At the same time, add rice to rice cooker and turn to cook.
  3. Add seasoning to wok and stir.
  4. Once rice is done, stir and let sit for 2-3 minutes, then adding soy sauce to taste.
  5. Mix curry powder with water in pot and stir on high heat until desired consistency.
