Honey Chilli Beef Noodles

Description

Beef & noodles mixed with honey & sweet chilli sauce.

Ingredients

  • 400g beef
  • 2 nests noodles
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2tsp onion salt
  • 2tsp garlic powder
  • 0.5tsp ginger
  • 0.25tsp chilli flakes
  • 2tbsp soy sauce
  • 1tbsp honey
  • 1.5tbsp sweet chilli sauce

Steps

  1. Cut veg & beef and add to wok. While cooking add boiling water to noodles in pot and leave to boil.
  2. Once cooked add seasonings, soy sauce, honey & sweet chilli sauce.
  3. Add noodles to wok and stir well.
