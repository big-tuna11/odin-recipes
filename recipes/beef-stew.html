Beef Stew

Description

A delicious homemade beef stew, slow cooked with carrots & potatoes in a rich gravy

Ingredients

  • 400g beef
  • 2 medium potatoes
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 1tsp lazy garlic
  • 1tbsp tomato puree
  • 500ml beef broth
  • 100ml chicken broth
  • 1tsp thyme
  • 1.25tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 bay leaves

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add onions, garlic, tomato puree & thyme. Cook for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth and stir. Transfer to slow cooker.
  3. Add beef broth, bay leaves & soy sauce to slow cooker.
  4. Season beef with salt & pepper. Add beef & carrots to slow cooker.
  5. Leave to cook for ~10 hours. Add potatoes for final 2 hours.
  6. Add cornflour mix or gravy granules to thicken broth then serve.
